About Our Project

Welcome to the Nutrition Tracker! Our project is designed to help you keep track of your daily food intake and provide insights to improve your diet and lifestyle. By logging your food consumption and maintaining an updated profile, our AI assistant will analyze your habits and offer personalized advice. Whether you’re managing allergies, working on fitness goals, or just looking to eat healthier, we’re here to support your journey to better health.

Features of our project include:

  • Log daily food and drink intake.
  • Track personal health stats like weight, height, allergies, and more.
  • Get personalized dietary suggestions from our AI assistant (coming soon!).
